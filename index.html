Fact About Dogs

Dogs are very friendly they are best friends of human dogs have a sense of time but don't know the 'concept' of the time.Unlike humans dogs don't have the ability to create the measure of time like the second,hour,minutes and they don't know how to read clock.

The dog is domesticated descendent of the wolf. Also called the domestic dog , it is derived from extinct Plestocine wolves , and the living wolf is the dogs nearest Relatives.
